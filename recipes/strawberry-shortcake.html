Strawberry Shortcake

Description

A combination of strawberry and creamy goodness all in one cake

This cake is made up of three main components: the cream & the cake (genoise) & the syrup

Ingredients

Syrup

Make a day ahead if possible
  • 60g cane sugar
  • 90g water

Genoise

  • 210g eggs (about 4 eggs)
  • 60g yolks (about 4 yolks)
  • 135g white sugar
  • 30g honey
  • 23g butter
  • 30g milk
  • 1/2 tsp. vanilla
  • 113g cake flour
  • 23 g cornstarch

Cream Ingredients
